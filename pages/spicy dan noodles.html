Spicy Dan Dan Noodles

Ingredients:

For Spicy Dan Dan Noodles:

  • 200g wheat noodles
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp sichuan peppercorns
  • 100g minced pork (optional)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp chopped peanuts
  • 2 scallions (chopped)
