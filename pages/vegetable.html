Vegetable Curry

Directions :

For Vegetable Curry :

  • Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  • Add mustard seeds and let them pop.
  • Add onions, garlic, ginger, and curry leaves, saut√© until golden brown.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, cumin, and mix well.
  • Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  • Pour in coconut milk and water, cover and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Add garam masala, mix well, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

For Rice :

  • Rinse the rice in water until the water runs clear.
  • Boil 4 cups of water in a pot and add salt.
  • Add the rice to the boiling water and stir.
  • Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the rice is cooked.
  • Fluff with a fork and mix in butter or ghee.

Ingredients :

For Vegetable Curry :

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, peas, beans, cauliflower)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Rice :

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
