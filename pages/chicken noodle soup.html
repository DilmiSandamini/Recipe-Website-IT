Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

For Chicken Noodle Soup:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (sliced)
  • 2 celery stalks (sliced)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

For Chicken Noodle Soup :

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)

Directions:

For Chicken Noodle Soup:

  • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
  • Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  • Add shredded cooked chicken, egg noodles, thyme, and bay leaf.
  • Reduce heat and simmer until noodles are tender (about 10 minutes).
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Remove bay leaf and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
