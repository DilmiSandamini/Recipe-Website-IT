Chicken Curry

Directions :

For Chicken Curry :

  • Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, cumin, and salt.
  • Cook until tomatoes soften and the oil separates.
  • Mix in chicken pieces and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add yogurt and stir well, cooking for another 5 minutes.
  • Pour in coconut milk (if using) and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

    • For Rice :

  • Rinse the rice in water until the water runs clear.
  • Boil 4 cups of water in a pot and add salt.
  • Add the rice to the boiling water and stir.
  • Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the rice is cooked.
  • Fluff with a fork and mix in butter or ghee.

Ingrediants :

For Chicken Curry :

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • 1 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Rice :

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
