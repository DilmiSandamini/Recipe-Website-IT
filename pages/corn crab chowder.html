Corn & Crab Chowder

Directions:

For Corn & Crab Chowder:

  • In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions, celery, and bell pepper. Sauté until soft (5–7 minutes).
  • Stir in minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  • Sprinkle flour over the vegetables and stir continuously for 1–2 minutes to make a roux.
  • Slowly add chicken broth while stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Add corn kernels, diced potatoes, Old Bay seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender (about 15 minutes).
  • Stir in heavy cream and lump crab meat. Simmer for 5 more minutes (do not boil).
  • Garnish with chopped green onions or fresh parsley before serving.
  • Serve hot with crusty bread or oyster crackers on the side.

Ingredients:

For Corn & Crab Chowder:

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
  • 2 medium potatoes (peeled & diced)
  • 1 tsp Old Bay seasoning
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup lump crab meat (picked over for shells)
  • Chopped green onions or parsley (for garnish)
