Dhal Curry

Directions :

For Dhal Curry :

  • Wash the lentils thoroughly.
  • In a pot, add lentils, water, turmeric, salt, and cook until soft.
  • In a separate pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds.
  • Once they pop, add chopped onions, garlic, curry leaves, and sauté until golden.
  • Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Pour this mixture into the cooked lentils and mix well.
  • Simmer for 5 minutes and add coconut milk.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

For Rice :

  • Rinse the rice in water until the water runs clear.
  • Boil 4 cups of water in a pot and add salt.
  • Add the rice to the boiling water and stir.
  • Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the rice is cooked.
  • Fluff with a fork and mix in butter or ghee.

Ingredients :

For Dhal Curry :

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dhal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Rice :

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
