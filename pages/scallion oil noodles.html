Scallion Oil Noodles

Directions:

For Scallion Oil Noodles:

  • Cook the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and add chopped scallions.
  • Fry the scallions until crispy and fragrant (about 3-5 minutes).
  • Remove the scallions and keep the infused oil.
  • Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and sesame oil to the pan.
  • Stir the sauce well and toss the noodles in the mixture.
  • Garnish with crispy scallions and serve hot.

Ingredients:

For Scallion Oil Noodles:

  • 200g wheat noodles
  • 4 scallions (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
