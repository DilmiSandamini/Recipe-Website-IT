Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

For Strawberry Cheesecake:

  • For the crust:
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • For the cheesecake filling:
  • 4 cups cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • For the strawberry topping:
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Directions:

For Strawberry Cheesecake:

  • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • line the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan with parchment paper.
  • For the crust, pulse graham crackers and sugar in a food processor until fine crumbs form.
  • Add melted butter.
  • Press the crust mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan and bake for 10 minutes.
  • Remove and let cool while preparing the filling.
  • In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  • Add sugar and vanilla extract, and mix until fully combined.
  • Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Reduce the mixer speed to low and add sour cream and heavy cream. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the cheesecake mixture over the cooled crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
  • Turn off the oven, crack the oven door slightly, and let the cheesecake cool for 1 hour.
  • Then transfer it to the fridge and chill for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  • For the strawberry topping, combine fresh strawberries and sugar in a small saucepan.
  • Cook over medium heat until the strawberries release their juices and the mixture thickens.
  • (about 5-7 minutes).
  • Let the strawberry topping cool before spreading it over the chilled cheesecake.
  • Serve chilled, garnished with additional fresh strawberries if desired.
