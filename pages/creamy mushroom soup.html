Creamy Mushroom Soup

Directions:

For Creamy Mushroom Soup:

  • Heat butter in a large pot over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  • Add sliced mushrooms and cook until they soften.
  • Sprinkle flour over mushrooms and stir well.
  • Gradually add vegetable broth while stirring continuously.
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then blend until smooth.
  • Stir in heavy cream, salt, and pepper.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Ingredients:

For Creamy Mushroom Soup:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 500g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
