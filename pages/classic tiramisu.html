Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients:

For Classic Tiramisu:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 500g mascarpone cheese (room temperature)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (whipped to stiff peaks)
  • 1 1/2 cups brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Optional: Shaved chocolate or cocoa nibs (for garnish)

For Classic Tiramisu :

  • 120g semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

Directions:

For Classic Tiramisu:

  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale.
  • In a separate bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese until smooth and creamy.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk the heavy cream to stiff peaks.
  • Fold the mascarpone cheese into the egg yolk mixture gently.
  • Incorporate the whipped cream into the mixture until smooth and creamy. Set aside.
  • In a shallow dish, pour the coffee (or espresso).
  • Quickly dip the ladyfingers into the coffee, making sure not to soak them.
  • Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish.
  • Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  • Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to let the flavors meld together.
  • Before serving, dust with cocoa powder and garnish with shaved chocolate or cocoa nibs if desired.
