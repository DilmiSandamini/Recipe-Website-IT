Sesame Noodles

Ingredients :

For Sesame Noodles :

  • 200g noodles (spaghetti or Asian noodles)
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions (sliced)
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • ¼ cup crushed peanuts (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

For Sesame Noodles :

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder

Directions :

For Sesame Noodles :

  • Boil a large pot of water and cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
  • Once cooked, drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set them aside.
  • In a small mixing bowl, whisk together **sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, minced garlic
  • If you like a little spice.
  • add red chili flakes or a dash of hot sauce to the sauce.
  • Place the cooled noodles in a large bowl and pour the sesame sauce over them.
  • Toss gently to coat the noodles evenly.
  • Add sliced **green onions and sesame seeds** for extra flavor and texture.
  • For a crunchier bite, mix in crushed peanuts or cashews.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves for a refreshing taste.
  • Serve cold or at room temperature. Enjoy as a main dish or a side dish!
