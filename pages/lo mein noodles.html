Lo Mein Noodles

Ingredients:

For Lo Mein Noodles:

  • 200g Lo Mein noodles
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • ½ cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 scallions (chopped)

For Mein Noodles :

  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)

Directions:

For Lo Mein Noodles:

  • Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  • then drain and set aside.
  • Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add carrots, bell peppers, and cabbage, stir-frying for 3 minutes.
  • Mix in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and hoisin sauce.
  • Add the cooked noodles and toss to coat with the sauce.
  • Garnish with chopped scallions and sesame seeds before serving.
