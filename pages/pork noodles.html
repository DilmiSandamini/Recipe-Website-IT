Pork Noodles

Directions :

  • Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water. Set aside.
  • Heat a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 tbsp of sesame oil.
  • Add the ground pork and cook for 5–7 minutes, breaking it into small pieces until browned.
  • Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, and sliced green onions to the pan. Stir-fry for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Stir well to coat the pork evenly.
  • Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together. Let them absorb the flavors for 2 minutes.
  • Sprinkle sesame seeds and red chili flakes for extra flavor.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious pork noodles!

Ingredients :

  • 200g egg noodles or ramen noodles
  • 250g ground pork
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 green onions (sliced)
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
