Peanut Noodles

Ingredients:

For Peanut Noodles:

  • 200g wheat noodles
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 2 tbsp chopped peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 scallions (chopped)

For Peanut Noodles :

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder

Directions:

For Peanut Noodles:

  • Cook the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • In a bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, and garlic.
  • Heat a small pan and lightly saut√© the sauce for 1-2 minutes.
  • Toss the cooked noodles with the peanut sauce until well coated.
  • Garnish with chopped scallions, crushed peanuts, and sesame seeds.
  • Serve warm or chilled as desired.
