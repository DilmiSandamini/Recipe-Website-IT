Chocolate Lava Cake

Directions:

For Chocolate Lava Cake:

  • Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F) and grease 4 ramekins with butter, dust with cocoa powder.
  • Microwave chopped chocolate and butter in 20-second bursts until smooth. Cool slightly.
  • Whisk eggs, yolks, sugar, and salt until pale and frothy (about 2 mins).
  • Slowly mix melted chocolate into egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  • Sift in flour, fold gently until incorporated.
  • Divide batter among ramekins, filling 3/4 full.
  • Bake for 12–14 minutes, until edges are firm but the center is soft.
  • Let cool for 1 minute, run a knife around edges to loosen, then invert onto plates.
  • Serve warm with powdered sugar, berries, or vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients:

For Chocolate Lava Cake:

  • 120g semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing ramekins)
  • Powdered sugar or vanilla ice cream (for serving)
