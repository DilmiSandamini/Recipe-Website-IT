Mango Sticky Rice

Ingredients:

For Mango Sticky Rice:

  • 1 cup sticky rice (glutinous rice)
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • Optional: Sesame seeds or mung beans for garnish

For Coconut Sauce :

  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch or tapioca flour
  • A small pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)

Directions:

For Mango Sticky Rice:

  • Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • Soak the rice for at least 1 hour or up to 4 hours.
  • Drain the rice and steam it in a bamboo or metal steamer over simmering water.
  • for about 20-30 minutes, or until tender.
  • While the rice is steaming, combine coconut milk, sugar, and a pinch of salt in a saucepan over medium heat.
  • Stir until the sugar dissolves, then remove from heat.
  • Once the sticky rice is cooked, transfer it to a mixing bowl and pour the coconut milk mixture over it.
  • Stir gently to combine, then let the rice sit for about 10 minutes to absorb the coconut milk.
  • Peel and slice the mango into thin slices, removing the pit.
  • To serve, scoop some sticky rice onto a plate, arrange mango slices on top or beside the rice.
  • drizzle with a little extra coconut milk (optional).
  • Garnish with sesame seeds or mung beans for crunch (optional).
