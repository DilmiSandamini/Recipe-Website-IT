Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients:

For Tomato Basil Soup:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 5 large tomatoes (chopped) or 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

For Tomato Basil Soup :

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)

Directions:

For Tomato Basil Soup:

  • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  • Add onions and garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Pour in broth and bring to a simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Blend the soup until smooth using a blender or immersion blender.
  • Stir in heavy cream (if using), oregano, salt, and pepper.
  • Add fresh basil leaves and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot with Parmesan cheese and crusty bread.
